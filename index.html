ABOUT US

CodeSutra is a global educational initiative to train , inspire , aware and group students and software engineers from across the globe who are interested in competing and improving by participation in various competition and interestingly designed contests of domestic, national and international level.

We provide a platform for programmers/coders to practice , discuss and show off their skills in their suitable computer programming language . We test the students on the basis of their monthly and yearly contests and competition to check and optimise their skills to the fullest of their efficiency. The test are designed under the guidance of known and experienced coders and educational firms.
